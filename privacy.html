Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

This application ("Meta Ads Fetcher") accesses the Meta Ad Library API to retrieve publicly available advertising data.

This app accesses only publicly available ad data from the Meta Ad Library. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data retrieved from the Meta Ad Library API is used solely to display ad information to the authenticated user. No data is stored on our servers.
